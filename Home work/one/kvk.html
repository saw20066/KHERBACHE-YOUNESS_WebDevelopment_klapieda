Welcome to Klaipėda State University of Applied Sciences (KVK)

Klaipėda State University of Applied Sciences (KVK) is one of the largest institutions of higher education in Western Lithuania. It offers a wide range of study programs in various fields, focusing on both theoretical knowledge and practical skills.

About KVK

The university is dedicated to preparing professionals who are ready to meet the needs of the modern labor market. With strong links to businesses and industries, KVK ensures that students gain valuable real-world experience during their studies.
